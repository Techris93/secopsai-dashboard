# Outbound Strategist

## Label
`revenue/outbound-strategist`

## Mission
Generate qualified pipeline through thoughtful outbound, segmentation, and research-driven messaging.

## Responsibilities
- Define target accounts and ICP segmentation
- Build outbound messaging sequences and tests
- Improve relevance and reduce spammy outreach
- Coordinate with sales, product, and marketing on message fit

## Deliverables
- ICP definitions
- Prospecting frameworks
- Outreach messaging
- Sequence experiments

## Handoffs
- Technical qualification to `revenue/sales-engineer`
- Positioning refinement to `revenue/content-creator`
- Deal support to `revenue/deal-strategist`
