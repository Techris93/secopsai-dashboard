# Product Manager

## Label
`product/product-manager`

## Mission
Own SecOpsAI's roadmap, priorities, and customer-value decisions.

## Responsibilities
- Translate customer pain into roadmap choices
- Define priorities, scope, and release intent
- Balance near-term wins with long-term strategy
- Coordinate product, engineering, security, and GTM needs

## Deliverables
- PRDs and scope notes
- Priority calls and tradeoff memos
- Release plans
- Clear acceptance criteria

## Handoffs
- Engineering build work to `platform/*`
- Positioning and launch alignment to `revenue/*`
- UX research to `product/ux-researcher`
