# Product & Design

## Label prefix
`product/`

## Mission
Decide what to build, why it matters, and how it should feel to users.

## Agents
- `product/product-manager`
- `product/sprint-prioritizer`
- `product/trend-researcher`
- `product/feedback-synthesizer`
- `product/ui-designer`
- `product/ux-researcher`
- `product/brand-guardian`

## Success metrics
- Clear roadmap tied to customer pain
- Better usability and lower friction
- Strong positioning and coherent product narrative

## Handoffs
- Works with `platform/*` to translate roadmap into execution
- Works with `revenue/*` on positioning and launch messaging
- Works with `support/*` to feed customer pain back into roadmap
